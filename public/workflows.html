The 3-Task Day

Focus on three critical tasks each morning. Everything else is noise. This workflow strips away the illusion of productivity and forces you to ship what matters.

Best for:Founders, solopreneurs, anyone drowning in endless to-do lists.

Inbox-to-Zero

Transform every incoming request into a decision: do, delegate, defer, or delete. No orphaned tasks, no mental clutter. Your inbox becomes a processing machine.

Best for:Managers, support teams, anyone juggling constant inbound requests.

Project Ladder

Break large projects into vertical milestones. Each rung is a shippable increment. Climb one step at a time without losing sight of the summit.

Best for:Product teams, agencies, anyone building complex deliverables over weeks or months.

Weekly Command Review

Every Friday, audit your week. What shipped? What stalled? What needs to die? This workflow turns reflection into a tactical advantage for the week ahead.

Best for:Executives, strategists, anyone who needs to see the forest and the trees.

Questions, answered.

Clear expectations before you reach out.

How quickly can I start?

You can be up and running in minutes. TaskFlow is designed for immediate use—no complex setup or training required. Create your first task list, organize by priority, and start tracking progress right away.

Will it work for personal tasks and goals?

Absolutely. TaskFlow adapts to any workflow—whether you're managing personal projects, household tasks, or long-term goals. The flexible structure lets you organize work exactly how you think.

How do I keep it from getting complicated?

Start simple and expand only when needed. TaskFlow grows with you—use basic lists at first, then add labels, due dates, or subtasks as your needs evolve. The interface stays clean no matter how much you track.

What's the best workflow for busy weeks?

Focus on priority views and daily planning. Use filters to see only what matters today, defer non-urgent items, and batch similar tasks together. TaskFlow's quick capture ensures nothing falls through the cracks when time is tight.

Can I adapt it to recurring routines?

Yes. Set up templates for repeating workflows—weekly reviews, monthly goals, daily routines. Clone task lists or use recurring task patterns to maintain consistency without manual re-entry.

How do I get help setting it up?

We provide guided onboarding, video tutorials, and template libraries to help you configure TaskFlow for your specific needs. Our support team is available via email and chat to answer questions as you build your workflow.
